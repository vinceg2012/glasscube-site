Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Overview

GlassCube AR ("the App") is developed by Vincent Galeano. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

GlassCube AR does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App does not require an account, and no usage data is transmitted to external servers.

Camera & AR Access

The App uses your device's camera solely to provide the augmented reality experience. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Analytics

The App does not include any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our support page.
